Project Melody is a student run organization dedicated to spreading the love of music.

Mission Statement

Specific Goals
  1. Provide musical help and learning to younger and less experienced musicians.

  2. Provide an inclusive and fun community where youth can play music together.

  3. Spread love and happiness through our passion for music.

  4. Have plenty of performance opportunities every year at nursing homes, holiday events, ACAN events, etc.

  5. Hold an annual youth concert/contest where all musicians interested can participate.

Mission Statement:

Our goal is to allow youth with passion for music to spread their talent and create an engaging environment for our community.
